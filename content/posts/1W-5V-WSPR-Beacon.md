I have designed, built, and tested an all-HF WSPR beacon that produces 1W @ 5V
and is rugged and cost-effective.

![1W WSPR Beacon Board](/images/1W-5V-Beacon.png)

## Results

Power consumption: Around 420mA @ 5V.

## Cost

Cost: Around 500 INR.

## Time to build

Time to build: Less than 30 minutes.

## Files

The files for this project are [published here](https://github.com/kholia/HF-PA-v10) for personal (non-commercial) use.

For a 5W (QRO) WSPR beacon, please stay tuned for our upcoming QEX magazine
article ;)
